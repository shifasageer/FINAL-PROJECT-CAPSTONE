Our Mission

Emergency SOS & Safety Tips

Our mission is to ensure that everyone has quick access to emergency services. The app features an Emergency SOS service that sends instant messages to your trusted contacts, providing them with your location and a pre-set emergency message.

Additionally, our Safety Tips Course empowers users with essential knowledge on how to stay safe in various situations, providing you with the tools you need to protect yourself and your loved ones.

Emotional Well-being Tracking

We are committed to helping you achieve emotional balance and mental well-being. The app tracks various aspects of your daily life such as sleep patterns, screen time, diet, and work-life balance, offering insights and reminders to help you maintain a healthier lifestyle.

With personalized alerts and recommendations, our goal is to support your journey to emotional well-being, making sure you take the time to care for your mind as much as your body.
